The Firm

PMS AVOCATS assists companies with all their corporate, commercial, and tax issues, providing both advisory and litigation services in the public and private sectors. The firm positions itself as a strategic partner in the economic growth of its clients, both in France and internationally.

The firm and its network also represent clients before criminal courts in economic and financial matters, as well as in general criminal law, with a particular commitment to defending whistleblowers and individuals involved in national political life.

Practice Areas

Corporate Law · White-Collar Criminal Law · General Criminal Law

Our lawyers primarily intervene, whether as plaintiffs or defendants, in the following areas:

  • Corporate Law: company law (incorporation and dissolution of companies, articles of association, shareholders' agreements, shareholder disputes, transfer of shares, transfer of businesses, holding companies, and group restructuring), contract law (drafting, negotiation, liability), civil liability and directors' and officers' liability (D&O), commercial litigation, unfair competition and passing off, tax advisory and litigation, insolvency proceedings, transfer of going concerns, and transfer of leasehold rights.
  • White-Collar Criminal Law: breach of trust, misuse of corporate assets, fraud, fraudulent bankruptcy, forgery and use of forged documents, corruption and unlawful taking of interest, tax fraud, money laundering, securities offenses, cybercrime (particularly related to cryptocurrencies or crypto-assets), and corporate criminal liability.
  • General Criminal Law: felonies and misdemeanors against persons, traffic offenses, acts of violence, etc.; felonies and misdemeanors against property; offenses against the nation, the State, and public peace.

Founder and Paraclet Network

After working in-house as the legal director of an incubator and within the judiciary – in a correctional chamber and a specialized section of the Public Prosecutor's Office against organized crime – Mathieu SASSI was admitted to the Paris Bar and founded PMS AVOCATS and the Paraclet network.

PMS AVOCATS and the professionals of the Paraclet network (lawyers, legal counsels, chartered accountants, bailiffs, associations) practice their profession with a specific mindset: one of justice, conciliation, and, when necessary, strength, to defend the client's interests.

The founder, Mathieu SASSI, holds a degree in private law from Paris-Sorbonne University and a degree in security and crisis management from the Institute for International and Strategic Affairs (IRIS). He also taught corporate law for several years at the Catholic University of Paris.
